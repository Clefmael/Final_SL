Our Story

From a small idea to a nationwide movement for educational equity.

EducateLanka was born from a shared dream among a group of friends who believed that every child in Sri Lanka, regardless of their background, deserves access to quality education that prepares them for the future.

We started in a small community center with a handful of students and a passion for teaching. Today, we've grown into a trusted organization, connecting dedicated volunteers and generous donors with thousands of aspiring students across the island.

Our mission is to bridge the educational gap by providing essential skills in English, Information & Communication Technology (ICT), and career development, fostering a generation of confident and capable leaders.
